Ever since I was little I have loved being outdoors basically doing whatever my dad liked to do. I learned everything from my dad and as I got older I took on new hobbies as I was ready to. I started out fishing and skiing when I was very young because those were things I was ready to learn. I wasn't allowed to go out hunting until I was ten years old because that was the legal starting age for kids in Maine. But, I remember always begging my dad to take me out hunting with him when I was about five because all of my cousins and and uncles would come over and meet at our house before going out for the day. Looking back I find this memory commical because I had no idea what they were actually going to do at the time I just knew that if my dad was going I wanted to go with him and find out. My dad also found this funny because he spent years trying to get my brother and mother to want to go out hunting with him and there I was five years old ready to go every Saturday morning trying to come along for the ride. I had to settle for truck rides on dirt roads, along big open fields just before dusk to only look at the deer. To everyone's surprise I took up hunting when I was ten and haven't stopped yet and honestly my favorite part about it is just being able to spend time with my family every weekend for a few weeks. After hunting I started cheering in the fifth grade and have continued to college. Recently I have taken up hiking and really enjoy it.
